Our Story

A Husband's Love
For His Wife

Luxy & Love was founded with one simple belief:

Every mother deserves healing, recovery, rest, and unconditional care after bringing new life into the world.

Our name reflects the deepest love a husband can give his wife, ensuring she receives comfort, nourishment, peace of mind, and professional support throughout her postpartum journey.

“At Luxy & Love, mothers are not simply cared for. They are cherished.”
